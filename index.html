FROSTBITE: The Story

FROSTBITE first embarked on their musical voyage of discovery together as a cohesive Rock Band in 2017. They where "reborn" early 2019 with some new members. Their origin goes back earlier to when the band members grew up listening to a variety of music genres that played an influential role in shaping their paths as musicians and ultimately led them together. As FROSTBITE, they have collectively found their signature sound, and offer something different and unique to their fans by creating and sharing original music they are proud to call their own.
